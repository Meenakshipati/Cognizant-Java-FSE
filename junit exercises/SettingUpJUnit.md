***# Setting Up JUnit***



***## Steps to set up JUnit in Maven Project***



***1. Open `pom.xml` file***

***2. Add JUnit 5 dependency:***

***```xml***

***<dependency>***

&#x20;   ***<groupId>org.junit.jupiter</groupId>***

&#x20;   ***<artifactId>junit-jupiter</artifactId>***

&#x20;   ***<version>5.10.0</version>***

&#x20;   ***<scope>test</scope>***

***</dependency>***
